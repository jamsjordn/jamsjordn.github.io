      Academic Writing

      9 Jul 17

      13:00-16:00

      TBA

      Type: single
      Tutor: Crime, Matthew
      Target Group: All UTS students
      What it covers: The workshop (total of 15 hours) will take you through the entire writing process, from analysing an assignment question to planning, researching, writing and editing your assignment draft. The skills that you will learn can then be easily transferred to your context of learning.

      Objectives:

      • To analyse assignment questions and plan for assignments
      • To understand different types of assignments and their structures
      • To practise academic reading and note-taking skills
      • To practise paraphrasing and synthesizing skills
      • To correctly use Harvard (UTS) referencing style conventions
      • To write clear academic paragraphs
      • To understand common sentence-level grammar errors
      • To edit and proofread your writing

      Oral Presentation

      16 Jul 17

      14:00-16:00

      TBA

      Type: single
      Tutor: Ethical, George
      Target Group: All UTS students
      What it covers: The workshop (total of 10 hours) aims to equip you with the skills to prepare for and deliver an effective presentation. Your verbal and non-verbal communication skills, and ability to use audiovisual aids appropriately will hopefully improve over the 5-day workshop. You will be given plenty of opportunities to present, and receive feedback from your presentations.

      Objectives:

      • To write a presentation objective and use it to focus as you prepare for your presentation
      • To select relevant material for your presentation
      • To express yourself clearly, audibly and fluently
      • To choose a speaking style appropriate to the situation
      • To use appropriate non-verbal communication
      • To use audio visual aids such as PowerPoint and Prezi to support your presentation
      • To handle questions from an audience
      • To put some strategies into place for overcoming nerves
      • To compose and deliver a short presentation and gain feedback from this presentation
      • To evaluate your own performance

      Places available: 50
